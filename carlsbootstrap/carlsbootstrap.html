About the owners:

Bruce Schollmann started repairing cars at his family body shop in his first year of high school and he has been working there since. He went from detailing cars to learning to repair small dings and dents, to mastering major collisions repairs and painting cars.

Bruce & Helen Schollmann took over running the family business in 2005 and have been going full force since, making changes and updating the business, without taking away from its small-town feel. When you come into Carl's your not just a number, you're one of the family. Customer service, quality and efficiency are our number one priority and we are always looking for ways to improve this business.

Carl's Body Shop is family owned and operated and has been serving Sonoma County since 1963. We have 50 years of family experience that shows in our work. Our focus has always been to provide vehicle owners with the finest quality and safest collision repairs.

Bruce completing a bumper replacement.



We at Carl's Body Shop are honest professionals who perform our job with enthusiasm and skill. We strive to make sure all your questions are answered and well explained throughout the entire process. Our goal is to ensure proper repairs and to restore your vehicle back to factory specifications. We know that your vehicle is one of your largest investments.

Carl's Body Shop is kind to our environment and a responsible member of the community. Carl's Body Shop went Green in 2009 using PPG waterborne paint, and Bruce and Helen are members of the local Chamber of Commerce and active members of the Rotary Club of Rancho-Cotati.
