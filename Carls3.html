About the owners:

Bruce Schollmann started repairing cars at his family body shop in his first year of high school and he has been working there since. He went from detailing cars to learning to repair small dings and dents, to mastering major collisions repairs and painting cars. Bruce & Helen Schollmann took over running the family business in 2005 and have been going full force since, making changes and updating the business, without taking away from its small-town feel. When you come into Carl's your not just a number, you're one of the family. Customer service, quality and efficiency are our number one priority and we are always looking for ways to improve this business.

Beyond just providing auto body collision repair, auto glass and paint for your car, we are a green business and do what we can to minimize our carbon footprint. We use a low-VOC European waterborne paint, recycle extra parts, shop materials and more. Come visit Body Best Collision Center to experience award-winning service within a clean, well-organized facility that is attentive to your car and to the environment. You will drive away with a car that looks and runs like new—we include a full detail with every repair to ensure this is so!
